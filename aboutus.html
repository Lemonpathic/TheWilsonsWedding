What's the temple for?

The temple holds a special place in our hearts, as it has been intertwined with the significant milestones of our relationship right from the beginning. Our first dates took place in the temple grounds, where we built a strong foundation of love and spiritual connection. It represents a sacred space where we feel a deep sense of reverence and unity. The temple symbolizes our shared values, beliefs, and aspirations.

For us, a temple marriage is about more than just a wedding ceremony. It is about the eternal family we are creating. We believe in the concept of an eternal family, where our love and commitment extend beyond this mortal life. Through the blessings and ordinances performed in the temple, we seek to be sealed together for time and all eternity. This eternal bond not only strengthens our relationship in this life but also ensures that our family will be united throughout all eternity. The temple serves as a gateway to the eternities, where we can continue to progress and grow together as an eternal family.
